## 3. Marketing Strategy

### Brand Positioning

**Tagline:** "Dance with your ancestors."

**Positioning Statement:** For urban Irish millennials and diaspora who feel culturally disconnected, RainbowFiddle is an AR cultural wellness platform that lets you experience Ireland's living mythology through movement and play—unlike passive museum visits or generic travel apps.

### Target Audiences

#### Primary: Urban Irish Millennials (22-38)
- **Demographics:** Dublin, Cork, Galway; moderate-to-high disposable income; active social media
- **Psychographics:** Seek authentic experiences; value heritage but feel disconnected; digitally native
- **Pain Points:** Loneliness, rootlessness, screen fatigue, meaningless content consumption
- **Channels:** Instagram, TikTok, Spotify podcasts, urban event sponsorships

#### Secondary: Irish Diaspora (25-45)
- **Demographics:** London, New York, Sydney, Toronto; professional class; passport holders
- **Psychographics:** Nostalgic, seeking connection to "home"; active in St. Patrick's Day culture
- **Pain Points:** Distance from heritage, lack of authentic cultural experiences abroad
- **Channels:** Irish diaspora Facebook groups, expat publications, heritage organizations

#### Tertiary: Cultural Tourists (25-40)
- **Demographics:** Visiting Ireland; seeking "off the beaten path" experiences
- **Psychographics:** Experience-seekers, willing to pay for authenticity
- **Channels:** TripAdvisor, Airbnb Experiences, travel influencers

### Marketing Funnel

```
AWARENESS (Top)
├── Instagram Reels (AR Cat dancing)
├── TikTok viral challenges
├── PR: Irish Times, Guardian, Wired
├── Podcast: Irish History, Culture
└── Heritage institution co-marketing

CONSIDERATION (Middle)
├── Landing page (www.rainbowfiddle.com)
├── App Store optimization
├── YouTube explainer video
├── User-generated content (UGC)
└── Beta waitlist / email capture

CONVERSION (Bottom)
├── Free experience (1 story)
├── In-app upgrade prompt
├── Social proof (NPS, shares)
└── Referral program (1 free month)

RETENTION (Loyalty)
├── Weekly push notifications (new stories)
├── Streak rewards (consecutive days)
├── Community events (Dublin meetups)
└── Annual "Mythic Pass" subscription
```

### Campaign: "Find the Cat"

**Launch Date:** June 2026 (Dublin, coinciding with Bloomsday)

**Phases:**

1. **Tease (Weeks 1-2):** QR codes appear around Dublin with "Something magical is coming." Social media speculation. Micro-influencers "discover" codes.

2. **Launch (Weeks 3-6):** App available. First 10,000 downloads get lifetime Mythic Pass. Press launch event at Temple Bar with AR demonstration. Live music, free cider.

3. **Amplify (Weeks 7-12):** UGC contest: Best AR Cat dance video wins a trip to the Aran Islands. TikTok challenge: #RainbowCatDance (target: 1M views).

4. **Sustain (Ongoing):** Weekly story drops. Seasonal content (Samhain encounter, Christmas magic). Partnership activations with Irish pubs worldwide.

**Budget Allocation:**
- Digital ads: 40% (€48K)
- Influencer partnerships: 25% (€30K)
- PR & events: 20% (€24K)
- Content production: 15% (€18K)
